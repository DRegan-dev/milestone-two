Welcome to Triviopolis

The city has been taken over by an Evil Entity that is making everyone sick. The streets are empty. The Hospitals are overun. Government refuses to take action. We need you. Defeat the Evil Entity by answering as many questions correctly as as you can within the time limit to save our people from this sickness. Make sure to hit the score goal every round or everyone you have saved will die.

Instructions:

  • Answer as many questions as possible in the alloted time.
  • Reach the Score Goal for each level in order to progress and save Triviopolis from the Evil Mayor.
  • Don't reach the goal and you will lose all of your progress.
